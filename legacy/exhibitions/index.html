Technical & Electrical Expertise

Designed, built, and powered to perform. Many exhibition challenges don’t come from design — they come from power, lighting, and technology failures.

Our team integrates technical planning from the earliest design stage, ensuring that:

  • Power requirements are correctly calculated
  • Lighting supports both architecture and brand presence
  • Screens, demos, and AV systems operate reliably
  • Venue electrical regulations are met without delays
  • Installation runs smoothly without last-minute fixes

This is especially critical for technology, energy, healthcare, and corporate exhibitors, where operational reliability is non-negotiable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle all electrical and technical works?

Yes. Electrical planning, installation, lighting, and AV coordination are part of our exhibition services. We handle everything from power distribution to LED screens and connectivity.

Do you manage venue approvals?

Yes. We handle technical drawings, submissions, and coordination with exhibition authorities including DWTC, Expo City Dubai, and ADNEC.

Can you work with tight timelines?

Yes. Our process is built around fixed exhibition deadlines. We understand that show dates don't move, and we plan accordingly with buffer time for unexpected challenges.

Do you provide installation and dismantling?

Yes. We manage full on-site execution and post-event dismantling. Our team supervises every phase from build-up to breakdown.

What size stands do you build?

We design and build stands from 36 sqm to 500+ sqm, including double-deck structures and country pavilions. Each project is custom-engineered for the specific venue requirements.
